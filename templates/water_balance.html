WATER BALANCE
What role does water play in human life?

It's no secret that water is the basis of life of any living being. Man is no exception. Blood, lymph, pancreatic and gastric juice, cytosol - the basis of all this is liquid. That is why it is so important to consume large amounts of water for the proper functioning of all organs and systems and maintaining electrolyte balance.

Symptoms of negative water balance:

  • Dry skin
  • Eruptions on the skin
  • Edema. Accumulation of water in various tissues and cavities
  • Thirst and dry mouth from lack of saliva
  • Bad breath
  • Language furnishing
  • Deterioration of the brain: the manifestation of symptoms of depression, sleep disorders, attention
  • Joint pain and risk of muscle spasms
  • If there is not enough fluid in the body, this entails constipation and a constant feeling of nausea

A few tips for maintaining adequate water balance:

  • You can drink water at any temperature, distribute the required volume in equal portions throughout the day
  • Take a bottle of water with you for walks, runs
  • Drink water with meals
  • Drink fluids between meals
  • In water, to improve the taste, you can add any berries or fruits: lemon, raspberries, strawberries
