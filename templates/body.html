All about the structure of our body
Briefly about the main

Our body is very complex. The work of organs depends on both external and internal factors. The whole organism can be divided into several systems: circulatory, musculoskeletal, digestive, respiratory and nervous. And we need to take care of each of them.

Digestion
Responsible for the processing of food and the removal of harmful substances from our body
Skeleton
Responsible for our movements, straight back and growth
Breath
Responsible for metabolism in the body and blood oxygenation
Blood

The circulatory system is responsible for circulating blood throughout the body and transporting nutrients

Complexity

Responsible for the processing and exchange of information in the body

Muscular
Moves our body and keeps it in a standing position
