Tips for Maintaining a Healthy Lifestyle

Useful tips

  • In the morning, as soon as you wake up, it is recommended to drink a glass of water, as well as before each meal (you can add lemon to the water)
  • As trite as it may sound, exercising in the morning will help you feel alert all day (verified)
  • It is worth eating more often 5 times a day, frequent snacking is harmful
  • Do not forget about eye health, do exercises to relieve eye strain
  • Watch your posture (it's more important than it seems)

Don't forget to

  • Get out in the sun more, but don't forget your sunscreen
  • Eat foods in all colors of the rainbow
  • Cardio training 1-2 times a week
  • Drink less coffee (you can replace it with green tea)
  • Walk more
