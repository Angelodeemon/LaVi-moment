Hygiene in human life

Briefly about the main

Since childhood, we have been constantly told how important it is to wash our hands, wash our face, brush our teeth properly, and so on. This is true, because hygiene is the key to a healthy body.

Keeping the skin in a healthy state requires constant care for its cleanliness and hardening. Cleanliness of the skin is the main condition for its normal condition. In the surface layer of the skin — the epidermis — there are a large number of sweat glands and hair follicles, in which dust, dirt, and bacteria are trapped. In addition, metabolic products secreted by the skin itself (fat, sweat, horny scales) constantly accumulate there. All this must be systematically washed off.


It is necessary to wash the skin taking into account its individual characteristics. So, for normal and oily skin, it is advisable to use neutral toilet soap for washing, and for dry skin — cosmetic, glycerin, etc. It is recommended to take a bath and shower at least once a week under normal conditions, and daily in environmentally unfavorable conditions.

! The skin of the face and neck should be washed in the morning, evening and as needed (sweating, pollution)

! The skin of the hands should be washed with soap and water at room temperature before each meal, after using the toilet, playing sports and being outside

! It is advisable to wash the skin of the legs every evening after a working day

Dental hygiene

Oral care is primarily about keeping your teeth healthy. When it is insufficient, dental diseases can develop. One of them is caries. The main reason for its occurrence is considered to be the destructive effect of plaque microorganisms on the hard tissues of the teeth.


You can prevent cavities by brushing your teeth regularly. It is necessary to clean them after breakfast and especially carefully (at least 3 minutes) after dinner. This should be done with a brush with artificial bristles.


In the intervals between meals and before going to bed (after brushing your teeth), you should avoid the consumption of sweets and starchy foods. It is necessary to strive to ensure that the teeth are always cleaned of food debris (brush after breakfast and dinner, rinse after any meal). To prevent diseases, it is recommended to visit a dentist at least twice a year.

Hair hygiene

General recommendations on what is beneficial to wash hair imply the use of nourishing shampoos (for example, based on oils), conditioners, masks, leave-in creams, or sprays. Homemade oil-based wraps can be done just before washing.

Special attention should be paid to split ends. It is important to protect the locks from harmful external influences. You should not stay under the scorching sun without a head covering, just as you should not neglect a warm winter hat in cold weather. For coloring, compositions without ammonia should be chosen.

In order to achieve healthy, thick, shiny locks without brittleness and split ends, it is necessary to adhere to general recommendations that will help prevent many problems:
  • Use suitable shampoos and conditioners
  • Give priority to combs with natural teeth
  • Do not neglect regular application of masks along the entire length of the hair
  • Try to use thermal electrical appliances less often
  • Timely treatment of skin diseases, including getting rid of dandruff
  • Periodically trim the ends
  • Do not comb immediately after washing, you need to let the hair dry naturally for a bit
Normal hair type:
In this case, the head usually only becomes dirty after 2-3 days. Combing is not particularly difficult, and split ends are also rarely a concern. Care is uncomplicated, and styling and shaping the locks into a hairstyle is also easy. Usually, this picture is characteristic of young girls who do not have harmful habits and lead a generally healthy lifestyle.
Hygiene involves regularly using shampoo, conditioner, mask, and periodically taking vitamin and mineral complexes. If these simple rules are neglected, the condition of the hair will deteriorate significantly very soon, and more serious methods of restoration will have to be used.
Dry hair type:
Taking care of such hair is slightly more difficult than in the previous case. Care procedures should be gentle and careful to prevent:
  • Dullness, lack of shine
  • Brittleness, fragility
  • Difficulty combing
  • Tangling
  • Split ends
  • Periodic appearance of dandruff
Oily hair type:
Its owners sometimes experience strong discomfort, as the overactive sebaceous glands quickly spoil the appearance of the locks. They seem unwashed, shiny, dense to the touch, elastic and difficult to style. In this case, not only the sequential stages of proper care for the scalp and hair play a key role, but also the quality of the diet (salt, spicy, animal fats should be minimized).
Mixed (combination) hair type:
The process is quite complicated because the hair at the roots remains oily while the ends are brittle and dry. Hygiene should be comprehensive and address several issues at once: reduce the intensity of sebum production and minimize the harmful effects on the ends. For example, shampoos are mainly applied to the root area, while masks and conditioners are applied to the rest of the length.
